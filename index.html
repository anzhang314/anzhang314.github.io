Biography

I am a Research Fellow in National University of Singapore, where I am a member of NExT++ Lab, supervised by Prof Tat-Seng Chua. With my colleagues, students, and collaborators, we strive to develop trustworthy and Robustness algorithms with better interpretability, generalization, and causality. Our research is motivated by, and contributes to, applications in information retrieval (e.g., collaborative filtering), data mining (e.g., graph pre-training), causality (e.g., causal inference and causal discovery). My work has several publications in top-tier conferences (e.g., NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, SIGIR) and journals (e.g., TPAMI). Moreover, I have served as the PC member for top-tier conferences including NeurIPS, ICML, and WSDM.

Prospective Intern Students

I am looking for highly motivated interns (visiting PhD, master, undergraduate students) to work together on trustworthy and robustness on graph, especially pre-training, interpretability, generalization, and causality, and their applications in real-world scenarios.
